Catching Up to Hope

We are not only reaching the tiny middle class of Cambodia. We are reaching the most impoverished children with our innovative Catch Up School program.

There are over 400 children catching up to their age appropriate grade in Asian Hope Catch Up Schools in 4 impoverished communities. 140 more youth are studying English - the international language of business. Everyone of them and their families are face to face with Jesus every day through our educators, the Biblically based curriculum, and partnership with the local Church.

These are children that have been locked out of the public schools due to corruption, poverty, and abuse. Without Catch Up School, they are trapped in the poverty cycle with no hope of escape.

We don’t lock our gates every evening and keep out the community. We fling open the gates and the children stampede onto campus with wide smiles and infectious laughter. Our new Asian Hope International School campus will provide evening Catch Up School to 250 additional un-schooled children living in the community surrounding the campus.

Without this new campus, these children will not be able to catch up and graduate from high school.

Without this new campus, these children won’t experience the thrill of a super fast circular slide on top quality playground equipment.

Without this new campus, these children won’t learn how to swim with a swim instructor in a safe and clean swimming pool.

Without this new campus, these children will not be exposed to the technology necessary to function in the professional world.

Without this new campus, most of these children will not be introduced to Jesus.
